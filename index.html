📷 Photo Finder — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Summary: Photo Finder processes everything on your device. Your photos never leave your phone. We collect zero data.

1. Introduction

Photo Finder ("the App") is a mobile application that allows you to search your photo gallery using natural language descriptions. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App does not have user accounts, analytics, tracking, advertising, or any server-side components that process user data.

3. Photo Access

The App requests permission to access your device's photo gallery solely for the purpose of:

  • Scanning and indexing photos using the on-device MobileCLIP neural network
  • Displaying search results within the App

Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. All image processing (neural network inference, embedding generation, and similarity matching) happens entirely on your device using ONNX Runtime.

4. On-Device Processing

All core functionalities of the App, including photo analysis and search, are executed entirely on your device. The App relies on on-device machine learning models and local storage to analyze image content and process your search queries without transmitting your photos or text to external servers.

5. Network Usage

The App may use the internet connection only for the following purpose:

  • Language model download: Google ML Kit Translate may download a language model (~30 MB) when you first use the search feature in a non-English language (Russian, Ukrainian, or German). This is a one-time download of a machine learning model from Google's servers.

No user content, search queries, photos, or any personal data are ever transmitted over the network.

6. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Photo embeddings — Numerical vector representations of your photos (not the photos themselves), stored in a local SQLite database
  • ML Kit language models — Downloaded translation models, managed by Google ML Kit

All locally stored data can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit Translate for offline translation. Google ML Kit's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Note that ML Kit processes translations on-device and does not send your text to Google's servers.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — To access your photo gallery for indexing and search
  • ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION — To access photo metadata (not used for location tracking)
  • INTERNET — For one-time ML Kit language model download

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
